Particle Field

Flowing streams of particles that drift across the scene with soft trails. Not scattered randomly — they move with direction, like currents. Cursor creates a force field: push, pull, scatter. Particles burst on click and reform. Rendered as instanced points in R3F with bloom glow.

Wave Lines

Subtle horizontal lines that undulate slowly across the background, like a calm audio waveform or ocean surface. They respond gently to cursor Y-position — move your mouse up, the waves compress; move down, they spread. Pure ambient texture.

Floating Icons

Sleek, minimal SVG icons in frosted-glass containers. They materialize out of nothing, drift slowly, then dissolve. Not always visible — 2-3 on screen at a time, with a pool of 6-7 rotating in. Click one: it shatters into particles and feeds a quote into the terminal.

Terminal Console

Glass-morphism panel with backdrop blur. Slides up from bottom on first interaction. Character-by-character typing. Accepts keyboard commands. Three dots at top like a native terminal window. Minimal, modern — not retro green-on-black.
