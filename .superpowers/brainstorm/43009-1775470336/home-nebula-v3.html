Nebula Interaction Model

  • Move cursor: Nebula displaces around it — smoke parting for your hand
  • Slow movement: A glowing tendril of particles trails behind with lag
  • Stop moving: Tendril diffuses back into the cloud over 2-3 seconds
  • Click: Shockwave ripples outward through the nebula
  • Idle 5s+: Nebula wisps curl toward cursor, as if curious
  • Click icon: Icon dissolves into nebula particles, quote types in terminal
